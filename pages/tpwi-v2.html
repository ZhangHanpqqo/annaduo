these particles we immersed ver.2 (2026)

Multimedia live set with reactive audiovisuals, aluminum foil, and performance with yarn.

Performed in km28, Berlin, Germany; International Computer Music Conference 2026, Hamburg, Germany.

In this 30-minute set, memories of the duo, their travels, lived experiences, and friendship are woven together through blue-colored yarn. The yarn brings a sense of warmth and intimacy, evoking childhood, home, and the playful memory of cat’s cradle, where connection is formed through fragile patterns held between hands. Foil becomes a reflective mirror, catching and scattering an ocean of fleeting moments. As the blue yarn penetrates through the foil, it disturbs the surface like a touch on water, lifting ripples from within memory that continue to flow, lingeringly and tenderly...
